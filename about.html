About SCWK

SCWK stands for Scrum & Coffee with Karthik — a space where practical Agile thinking, Scrum discipline, and AI curiosity come together with real-world perspective.

Why I created SCWK

SCWK is my way of sharing practical insights on Agile, Scrum, delivery thinking, and how AI is reshaping the way we work — not from theory, but from lived experience.

My journey has not been a straight line. From working as an IT Consultant in Qatar to facing a life-changing accident and rebuilding my career, every chapter has shaped how I think about resilience, adaptability, and growth.

Today, I continue my journey in the IT space by combining structured Agile thinking with emerging AI capabilities — with a strong focus on clarity, execution, and practical value.

SCWK is where I turn that journey into useful, shareable insights — one idea at a time.
