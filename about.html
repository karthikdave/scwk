Meet Karthik

I’m Karthik — the mind behind SCWK (Scrum & Coffee with Karthik), a personal brand built to share practical, modern, and professional insights through clean visual storytelling and premium carousel experiences.

The SCWK Idea

SCWK was created as a focused platform to bring together thoughtful Agile perspectives, crisp communication, and content that is easy to consume yet meaningful in impact. The goal is simple: create content that feels modern, sharp, and useful — without noise.

What SCWK Stands For

Coffee: conversational warmth, relatability, and a human tone.
Karthik: the personal lens, voice, and experience behind the brand.
