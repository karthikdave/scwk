About SCWK

SCWK stands for Scrum & Coffee with Karthik.

It’s a space where I share practical insights on Agile, Scrum, and how AI is reshaping the way we work — not from theory, but from real-world experience.

My journey hasn’t been a straight line. From working as an IT Consultant in Qatar to facing a life-changing accident, and rebuilding my career — every step has shaped how I think about resilience, adaptability, and growth.

Today, I continue my journey in the IT space, bringing together structured Agile thinking and emerging AI capabilities.

SCWK is my way of sharing that journey — one insight at a time.

Think better. Build smarter.
