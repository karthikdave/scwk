About SCWK

Why SCWK exists

SCWK (Scrum & Coffee with Karthik) is a personal knowledge-sharing initiative built around one simple belief: Agile should feel practical, not overwhelming.

Through short, visually simple content formats, SCWK breaks down Scrum, Agile delivery, team collaboration, stakeholder alignment, and execution patterns into clear, usable insights.

Whether you're a Scrum Master, Business Analyst, Product professional, or delivery leader, SCWK aims to make learning lighter, sharper, and more applicable to day-to-day work.
