About Karthik & SCWK

Built from real-world delivery. Designed for practical Agile learning.

SCWK is more than a content label — it is a practical, personal learning space shaped by real project experience, delivery challenges, and a belief that Agile should feel simpler, clearer, and genuinely useful.

15+ Years in IT 7+ Years in BA & Scrum Agile • Delivery • Mindset

I’m Karthik Dave — a delivery-focused professional with experience across Business Analysis, Scrum, and modern Agile ways of working.

SCWK (Scrum & Coffee with Karthik) was created to make Agile learning feel lighter, more relatable, and easier to revisit — especially for professionals who want clarity without heavy theory.

Instead of long frameworks and complicated explanations, SCWK focuses on simple, visual, and practical content that helps professionals learn faster, remember better, and apply ideas with confidence.

Through SCWK Bites and SCWK Lites, I share quick Agile thoughts, practical delivery insights, and light-hearted learning moments designed for modern teams and busy professionals.
